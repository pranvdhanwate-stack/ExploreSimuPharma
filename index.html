SimuPharma creates physics-accurate digital twins of pharmaceutical instruments. Students operate these virtual instruments exactly as they would real ones, getting results that match real equipment output within <2% accuracy.

Fourier Transform Infrared Spectroscopy

Real Equipment Value: ₹8-25 Lakhs

Identify functional groups and molecular structures through IR absorption. Our FTIR simulation implements Lorentzian line shapes and Beer-Lambert law for accurate spectral generation across the full infrared range.

  • Full spectral range (400-4000 cm⁻¹)
  • Automatic functional group identification
  • Transmittance & Absorbance display modes
  • Peak labeling and annotation tools
  • Reference spectra comparison library

Gas Chromatography

Real Equipment Value: ₹12-35 Lakhs

  • Real-time separation visualization
